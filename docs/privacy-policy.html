Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AZ Photo LLC, operating as The Love Lens by Ariana ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our photography services and our internal business management software ("the App"). We are committed to handling your personal information with care and transparency.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to clients who book photography services with The Love Lens by Ariana, including individuals who submit inquiries, sign contracts, make payments, or receive communications from us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when booking or communicating with us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Session details — date, time, location, and shoot type
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe — we do not store raw card numbers)
  • Contract and booking records
  • Correspondence — emails and messages exchanged with us
  • Inspiration images or references you share with us for your session

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Confirm and manage your photography session
  • Send session reminders and logistical details via email or SMS
  • Process payments and track outstanding balances
  • Deliver your photo gallery
  • Communicate with you before, during, and after your session
  • Maintain internal business records for accounting and tax compliance
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. SMS Communications

If you provide a mobile phone number and consent to SMS, we may send you automated text messages including session reminders, location details, and payment reminders. These messages are sent using Twilio, a third-party SMS provider. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly.

5. Payment Processing

Payments are processed through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. When you make a payment, your financial information is transmitted directly to Stripe and is subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy at stripe.com/privacy. We do not store your full card number or banking credentials on our systems.

6. Gallery Delivery

Completed photo galleries are delivered through Pixiset, a third-party gallery hosting platform. Your name and email address are shared with Pixiset solely to grant you access to your gallery. Pixiset's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

7. How We Store Your Data

Client data managed through our internal App is stored locally on our business computer and is not uploaded to any public cloud server. Access is restricted to authorized personnel of AZ Photo LLC. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including for tax and legal compliance requirements. In Arizona, we are generally required to retain business records for a minimum of four years. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, subject to legal retention obligations.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
